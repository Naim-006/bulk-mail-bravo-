Dear Student,

University life often becomes fragmented across multiple groups, chats, and shared drives. Important deadlines, lecture notes, and announcements are easily missed.

DIU Class Tracker is built to solve this problem by bringing everything into one structured academic system.

Open DIU Class Tracker

Android app version is coming soon to Play Store.

What you get

  • All class schedules in one place
  • Deadline tracking for assignments and exams
  • Lecture notes, lab reports, and question banks
  • Structured resource system instead of scattered groups
  • Real-time notifications for important updates
  • Calendar-based academic overview

How it works

Students do not need to register or log in. Simply select your batch and section once, and everything is automatically organized for you.

Class Representatives (CRs) and administrators have secure login access to manage schedules, upload resources, and maintain real-time updates.

Why it matters

The goal is simple: remove confusion, reduce dependency on multiple group chats, and ensure that no academic update is missed.

Everything you need for your semester is organized, structured, and always accessible.
